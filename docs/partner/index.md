# EIXAM Connect SDK — Partner Documentation

This site is the partner-facing integration portal for EIXAM Connect SDK.

It is intentionally narrower than the full internal documentation set. It focuses on what a partner team actually needs to integrate the SDK into a Flutter host app and complete the required Android/iOS setup.

6. [Android Integration](android-integration.md)
7. [iOS Integration](ios-integration.md)
8. [Backend Integration](backend-integration.md)

## What is intentionally out of scope here

This partner site does **not** expose internal validation workflows, handoff notes, or experimental surfaces such as Guided Rescue Phase 1.

Use the full site for internal engineering material.
