EIXAM Connect SDK — Partner Documentation

This site is the partner-facing integration portal for EIXAM Connect SDK.

It is intentionally narrower than the full internal documentation set. It focuses on what a partner team actually needs to integrate the SDK into a Flutter host app and complete the required Android/iOS setup.
